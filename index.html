About

Product thinker.
Systems builder.

I'm Max — a Senior Product Owner with a track record of owning complex, high-scale enterprise platforms. At Bank of America, I led product for Trident, an enterprise-grade compliance and assessment platform inside Global Information Security. Before that, three years at EchoStar / DISH as Technical PO for the Care Agent Application.

I believe great products come from sitting at the intersection of technical depth and business clarity — and that's the lens I bring to everything I ship.
